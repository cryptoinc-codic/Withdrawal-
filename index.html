Institutional Withdrawal Guide

NEXUS SECURE TERMINAL v1.4.2

Minimum Balance Requirement

To process any withdrawal, your account must maintain a minimum total balance of $15,000.00 USD. This institutional threshold ensures adequate liquidity for settlement finality and counterparty risk management. Withdrawal requests will be automatically declined until the available balance meets or exceeds this requirement.

Withdrawal Structure & Fee Breakdown

Network Routing Fee: $2.50 flat rate per transaction (non-refundable, covers blockchain gas or payment processor costs).

Net Settlement Calculation: Final Credit = Withdrawal Amount - $2.50

Maximum Limits: Up to full available balance per transaction, subject to daily institutional caps (contact support for higher tiers).

Processing Time: BTC: 1-3 block confirmations (~10-30 minutes). PayPal: Near-instant after HSM finality.

Bitcoin Address Requirements

Only the following Bitcoin address formats are accepted:

  • Legacy (P2PKH): Starts with "1" (e.g., 1A1zP1eP5QGefi2DMPTfTL5SLmv7DivfNa)
  • P2SH (SegWit compatible): Starts with "3" (e.g., 3J98t1WpEZ73CNmQviecrnyiWrnqRhWNLy)
  • Native SegWit (Bech32): Starts with "bc1" (e.g., bc1qar0srrr7xfkvy5l643lydnw9re59gtzzwf5mdq)

Address length must be between 26-62 characters. The terminal performs real-time regex validation before authorization.

PayPal Payout Specifications

Enter the verified email address associated with the recipient's PayPal Business or Personal account. Funds are disbursed in USD and typically arrive within minutes after HSM signing. Ensure the email follows standard format (user@domain.com) and is linked to an active PayPal account to avoid settlement delays.

Settlement Timing & Finality

Bitcoin Network: 1-3 confirmations required for finality (~10-30 minutes). The terminal will display a 15-second secure enclave simulation while cryptographic signing occurs.

PayPal Instant Transfer: Near-instant but subject to PayPal's internal risk review (typically < 2 hours).

All transactions are final after HSM multi-signature approval and cannot be reversed.

Compliance & Regulatory Framework

Nexus Secure operates under full institutional compliance with FATF Travel Rule guidelines. All withdrawals are logged and reported to relevant authorities. Tier-1 KYC verification must be completed on your dashboard before any withdrawal. AML monitoring applies to all transactions exceeding $3,000 USD equivalent.

Security Protocols (HSM & Encryption)

Each withdrawal request is processed through our Hardware Security Module (HSM) cluster, utilizing:

  • AES-256-GCM for payload encryption
  • RSA-4096 for digital signatures
  • Ephemeral session keys per transaction
  • Real-time threat detection and anomaly scoring

The 15-second processing window represents HSM key derivation and multi-party computation (MPC) signing.
