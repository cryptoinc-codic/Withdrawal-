Institutional Withdrawal Guide

Minimum Balance Requirement

Nexus Secure enforces a strict institutional liquidity threshold of $15,000.00 USD across all client accounts. This ensures adequate collateral for settlement finality and counterparty risk management. Withdrawal requests will be rejected until the available balance meets or exceeds this minimum.

Withdrawal & Fee Structure

• Network Routing Fee: $2.50 (flat, non-refundable).
• Net Settlement = Withdrawal Amount – $2.50.
• Maximum single withdrawal: up to full available balance (subject to daily institutional limits).
• All transactions are final after HSM multi-signature approval.

Bitcoin Address Requirements

Only legacy (1...), P2SH (3...), and native SegWit (bc1...) formats are supported. Address length must be between 26–62 characters. The terminal performs real-time regex validation before authorization.

PayPal Payout Rules

Enter the verified email address associated with the recipient's PayPal business account. Instant settlement occurs after HSM finality. Ensure the email follows standard format (user@domain.com).

Settlement & Timing

BTC network: 1–3 block confirmations (~10–30 min). PayPal: near-instant but subject to clearing. Each withdrawal undergoes a 15-second secure enclave simulation for cryptographic signing.

Compliance & AML/KYC

All withdrawal requests are logged and reported to institutional compliance. Tier-1 KYC verification must be completed on your dashboard. The terminal enforces FATF travel rule protocols for crypto above $3,000.

Security Protocols

Hardware Security Module (HSM) signing, AES-256 encrypted payloads, and real-time threat detection. The terminal uses ephemeral session keys per transaction.
